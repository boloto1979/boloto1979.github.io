Who am I?

You can contact me by sending a private message on Twitter or LinkedIn.


Projects

PwnTTY

Pwntty is a toolkit written in Python to control TTY devices. The goal of this project is to be used to control and play with other players terminals in real-time CTFs like KoTH, Battleground (HTB), etc.

Em Poucos Bits

My primary YouTube channel focused mainly on teaching about computing internals, malware and low-level programming in an easy way (Portuguese only).

astreuzz

My secondary channel where I post things I learned and proofs of concept without much effort or fancy edits.
